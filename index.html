About

This is a thrilling logic puzzle event open to college students. Compete with the best and prove your skills!

Rules

  • Participants must be current college students.
  • Teams of 2 members allowed.
  • Mobile phones are not allowed during puzzle solving.
  • Winners will be decided based on accuracy and time.
  • The competition consists of 4 rounds with increasing difficulty.
  • Each round will have different types of logic puzzles.
  • Points will be awarded based on correct solutions and speed.
